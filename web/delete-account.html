Delete your account from the app (fastest)

  1. Open Catch Up Column and go to the Profile tab.
  2. Tap Delete account.
  3. Confirm. Your account and data are removed immediately.

This permanently deletes:

  • your profile (display name, photo, bio, email);
  • all posts and photos you created;
  • your uploaded images in storage;
  • your memberships in every group (with moderator handoff or group removal where you are the sole moderator).

Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email support@catchupcolumn.com from the email address on your account with the subject "Delete my account". We will verify you own the address and delete your account and associated data within 30 days.

What we retain

Once deletion completes we do not retain your profile or content. Transient operational logs that may contain your email are rotated automatically and are not used to reconstruct your account.
