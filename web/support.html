Common questions

How do I join a group?

Ask the group's creator for their invite code, then open the app and tap Groups → Join a group and enter the code.

When do editions come out?

Each group has its own publish day and time, set by its moderator. When an edition publishes, everyone in the group gets it in the app, by email, and by push notification.

I stopped getting edition emails / I get too many.

Every edition email has an unsubscribe link at the bottom for that group. You can also manage email delivery from the group's settings.

How do I delete a post?

Open the compose screen for that group before the edition publishes — you can edit or delete your post there. Once an edition is published, its posts are part of that issue.

How do I delete my account?

In the app: Profile → Delete account. This permanently removes your profile, your posts and photos, and your group memberships. If you can't access the app, see Data Deletion Request.
